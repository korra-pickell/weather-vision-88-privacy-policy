## Information the App uses

### 1. Data stored on your device

The App stores information **locally** on your phone or tablet using the operating system’s standard storage, for example:

- Saved cities and your active location choice  
- App settings (units, display options, accessibility-related preferences, and similar toggles)

**We do not copy this data to our own servers** because we do not operate app backends for that purpose. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, that local information is removed according to how your device handles app data.

### 2. Device location (optional, while using the App only)

If you grant permission, the App may access **device location only while the App is in use** (not continuous background location) to:

- Request current and forecast weather for your approximate position  
- Center or align radar and regional features near you  

Location-related data is sent **from your device** to **third-party weather and geocoding services** so they can return results. If you deny location permission, you can still enter or select **saved cities** manually.

### 3. Information processed when you use the App

When you use forecasts, location search, radar, or related features, your device sends requests over the internet. Those requests may include, as needed for the feature:

- **Coordinates** (if you use device location)  
- **Search text** (if you search for a place)  
- **Technical data** typical of HTTP requests (such as IP address as seen by the service provider—handled under those providers’ policies)

We do not use this information to build an advertising profile in the App.

The App relies on independent providers. Their use of data is governed by **their** policies and terms. Examples referenced in the App include:

| Service | Role | More information |
|--------|------|-------------------|
| **Open-Meteo** | Forecast, conditions, alerts (where available) | [open-meteo.org](https://open-meteo.org/) |

Other data sources (for example map or boundary data) may be credited in the App’s **Sources & credits** screen. If we add or change providers, we will update this policy when appropriate.
